About Me

I am currently a first year Ph.D. student at Cornell University being advised by Professor Rachit Agarwal.

My current research interests include distributed systems, datacenter networking and peer-to-peer systems.

My current research focuses on understanding the feasability requirements of resource disaggregated datacenters and constructing systems that can yield their benefits. I am currently working on methods for realizing storage disaggregation. To that effect, I am working on building a datacenter-scale storage management layer for disaggregated storage.
